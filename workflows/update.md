# Status Update Workflow

## Input
What shipped, what's in progress, what's next. Freeform is fine. If any of the three are missing, ask for them one at a time.

## Output

Save to `docs/update-[YYYY-MM-DD].md` (use today's date):

---
# Update — [Date]

## Shipped
- **[Feature or fix]:** [One sentence on what it does or what was fixed]
- **[Feature or fix]:** [One sentence]

## In progress
- **[Item]:** [Current status + ETA if known]

## Up next
- **[Item]:** [One-line description]

---

## Changelog entry
`[Date] — [Feature]: [One sentence description of what changed]`

## Stakeholder summary
[Sentence 1: what shipped and why it matters.]
[Sentence 2: a metric or signal showing impact, or "early signal" if too soon.]
[Sentence 3: what's coming next and when.]

## Tweet thread

**Tweet 1 (hook):**
[Lead with a user benefit or milestone. No "we shipped X" openers. Max 280 chars.]

**Tweet 2 (detail):**
[What shipped + the most interesting technical or product detail. Max 280 chars.]

**Tweet 3 (CTA):**
[Invite people to try it, follow for updates, or reply with feedback. Include link if applicable. Max 280 chars.]
---

After saving, reply: "Update saved to `docs/update-[date].md`"
